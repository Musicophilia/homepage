Meet Lisa!

Hello, World! Currently, I am a student at Stanford University and pursue a major in Computer Science.

Born and raised in Germany, I was fortunate to be a Hector-fellow, which allowed me to participate in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) enrichment program for six years. Since I deeply appreciate the opportunities I got, I want to give back by improving how the STEM subjects are taught in K-12 schools. In ninth grade, I started tutoring students in math and discovered my passion for teaching. Watching students learn and helping them to win back their interest for a subject is the most fulfilling experience I have ever had. I believe in education as an equalizer of opportunities, and hope that I can apply my technical skills and my teaching experience to change the way math and computer science are taught in schools.
