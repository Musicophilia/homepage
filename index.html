Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my website! I recently graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from Stanford, and will return to finish my Masters with a focus in Artificial Intelligence. Since I experienced myself how valuable education can be, I am passionate about innovating education. With the help of data analytics and machine learning, I believe that we can improve knowledge discovery and education for students of all ages. I also hope to inspire more girls to pursue a career in science or engineering!

Here on my website, I have collected a few projects I've worked on, and also some of my recordings on the piano or the harpsichard. Have fun exploring!
